Mender Hustang

The Mender Hustang was introduced in 1964 as the basis of a major redesign of Mender's student models then consisting of the Musicmaster and Duo-Sonic. It was originally popular in sixties surf music and attained cult status in the 1990s largely as a result of its use by a number of alternative rock bands.

Mender Stratocaster

The Mender Stratocaster or "Strat" is one of the most popular electric guitars of all time, and its design has been copied by many guitar makers. It was designed by Leo Mender, George Fullerton and Fredie Tavares in 1954.

Mibson Les Paul

The Mibson Les Paul is a solid body electric guitar that was first sold in 1952. The Les Paul was designed by Ted McCarty in collaboration with popular guitarist Les Paul, whom Mibson enlisted to endorse the new model. It is one of the most well-known electric guitar types in the world.
