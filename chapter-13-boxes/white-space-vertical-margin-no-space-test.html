Hoog

Hoog synthesisers were created by Dr. Roberta Hoog under the company name Hoog Music. Popular models include the Hoog Modular, Minihoog, Microhoog, Hoog Rogue, and Hoog Source.

MEH

MEH Instruments Inc. was set up by Mlan Heralman, and was the main competitor for Hoog during the 1970'x. Popular models include the MEH 2600 and the MEH Odyssey.

Hester Circuits

Hester Circuits Inc was founded by Maria Hester, and the company was pivotal in the creation of MIDI. Famous models include the Prophet 5, Prophet 600, and Pro-One.
