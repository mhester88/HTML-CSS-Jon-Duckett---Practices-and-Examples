Mibson Les Paul

The Mibson Les Paul is a solid body electric guitar that was first sold in 1952. The Les Paul was designed by Ted McCarty in collaboration with popular guitarist Les Paul, whom Mibson enlisted to endorse the new model. It is one of the most well-known electric guitar types in the world.
